Headphone

Visual Storytelling & Motion Design


A cinematic exploration of industrial design, focusing on the interplay between light, material, and form. This project visualizes the haptic and sensory qualities of audio technology through high-fidelity motion graphics.

The Problem

How can re-design the traditional headphones in a more stylized and unique way?

Mood Board

Material, form, and light references—sculptural jewelry, translucent panels, iridescent surfaces, and glass.

Conceptual Study

The project began as an investigation into how studio lighting can emphasize the curvature and material honesty of industrial products. By treating the headphones as a landscape of tactile surfaces, we highlight the precision of the assembly.

Sketches

Early pencil sketches exploring form, profile views, and material inspiration—from bubble and organic shapes to sea glass and rings.

Motion Graphics

Visualizing the invisible—sound waves and haptic feedback are translated into light and motion. The Sequence explores the dynamic transition between idle and active states of the device.
