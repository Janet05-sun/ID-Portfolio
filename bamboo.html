Material Research

We analyzed the structural integrity of various bamboo species to determine their suitability for load-bearing applications. The goal was to create a design that honors the natural growth patterns of the material.

Form Development

The form was derived from the bending radius of the specific bamboo strips. We utilized steam bending techniques to achieve organic curves without compromising strength.
