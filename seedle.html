Seedle

Interactive Experience Design

Featured at The Trinellial Exhibition 2025 @ RISD


Seedle is a project focused on digital-physical interaction, exploring how we can plant seeds of creativity through simple, intuitive gestures. Developed over a week of intense prototyping, it represents a synthesis of technical skill and visual storytelling.

Ideation

Seedle was born from a desire to bridge the gap between digital manipulation and physical tactility. We asked ourselves: how can we make the act of "planting seeds" of ideas feel as tangible and rewarding as gardening, but in a digital space? The "why" lies in our need for more human-centric digital tools that encourage play and organic growth.

The process involved rapid prototyping with p5.js and physical sensors. We focused on the "flick" gesture—a motion that is both intuitive and satisfying. By mapping physical acceleration to digital velocity, we created a seamless transition from the real world to the screen. Every interaction was refined to ensure it felt responsive and alive.

Seedle is an immersive installation where users use a custom-built handheld device to "throw" seeds onto a large-scale projection. Once planted, these digital seeds grow into unique generative flora based on the velocity and angle of the throw. It's a living canvas that evolves with every participant, turning collective action into a blooming digital garden.
